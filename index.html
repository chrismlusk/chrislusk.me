ABOUT

I enjoy experimenting and exploring creative ways to create digital experiences that enhance meaning, motivation and context. I believe that designers and developers are essential to storytelling and should be actively involved in the process — from planning to presentation.

Based on strategy
Driven by creativity

I have worked as a print and digital designer at the Orange County Register, an online editor at The Oklahoman, and a copy editor at the Dallas Morning News. I graduated in 2012 from the University of Oklahoma, where I was editor of the campus newspaper, The Oklahoma Daily. This job introduced me to the visual side of journalism, and I became enthralled with the power of design. I began teaching myself web design in my free time after graduation and, since then, my interest level has grown so much that I am now studying programming at Coding Campus.
