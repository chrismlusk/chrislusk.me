ABOUT

I enjoy exploring creative ways to create digital experiences that enhance meaning, motivation and context. I believe designers and developers are essential to storytelling and should be actively involved in the process — from planning to presentation.

Based on strategy
Driven by creativity

After six years of professional and student journalism experience, I am now enrolled at Coding Campus studying programming. I have worked as a designer and copy editor at the Orange County Register, Dallas Morning News and The Oklahoman. I graduated in 2012 from the University of Oklahoma and was editor-in-chief of the campus newspaper, The Oklahoma Daily.
